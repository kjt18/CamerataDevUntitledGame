We are a small, 4 person development team that have created a new way to experience Rouge-like games for our Capstone project. In addition to the traditional Rouge-like experience, we have implemented online functionality, allowing you to compete against another players in real time. Learn more about the game below.

GameName

Rogue-like Online Multiplayer Game


In this competitive multiplayer Rouge-like, players will be able to challenge each other from any web browser. The traditional rules of a Rouge-like are found here, with a twist. Similar to sending an opponent lines in tetris, players will earn points while making their way through the dungeon floors. Their points will be compared and the loser will have to endure a higher difficulty, such as a larger horde or more powerful monsters. Below you will find the descriptions of the enemies and tools you may encounter while exploring the dungeons.

How to play

Player Movement:

The player can control their character's movement using the arrow keys. Conversely, the 'H', 'J', 'K', and 'L' keys for left, up, down, and right movements respectfully. The 'Y' and 'U' keys control left and right upward diagonal movements, and 'B' and 'N' for downward diagonal movements.

Player Actions:

To attack an enemy, walk into them. Picking up items off the ground is done by pressing the 'g' key while standing over an item. The inventory can be opened by pressing the 'i' key, and closed with the 'esc' or any directional key. Items can be removed from the inventory using the 'd' key. When the stairs to the next level of the dungeon are found, press '>' to descend.
